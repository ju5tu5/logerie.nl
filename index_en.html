Hotel De Logerie – Hospitality on the Dutch Countryside

Experience the tranquillity and hospitality of the Dutch Countryside at de Logerie. This luxurious bed and breakfast is located at the outskirts of Enkhuizen and offers the comfort of a three/four star Hotel. The spacious and elegant rooms with views of the garden and forest have a private en suite. Because of its unique location at the hart of Seed Valley on the Dutch countryside De Logerie is a ideal starting point to explore the surroundings or to go on a bike or hike trips. Every morning we will serve a varied fresh breakfast buffet for you to enjoy. Besides free Wi-Fi we offer free car and bike parking on site.

We are looking forward to welcome you at De Logerie.

Hotel · Bed & Breakfast

Rooms

The flowers we grow are our inspiration for the decoration of our five luxurious rooms. All rooms are spacious and have a an suite bathroom with shower or bad, toilet, sink, hairdryer, towels and toiletries. Comfortable soft beds (90 x 210) will make sure you can start your day relaxed. The five stylish and spacious rooms have views over the garden, a well-lighted desk, flat-screen television, sitting area and access to Wireless internet. With a delicious breakfast consisting of freshly baked bread, fresh orange juice, several fillings, home made jam, Milk, Yogurt, Muesli and eggs we will make sure your stay will be as perfect as possible.

Groups

The Accommodation

De Logerie is suited for groups of maximum 14 persons. The spacious five bedrooms with views of the garden and forest have a an suite bathroom with shower or bad, comfortable soft beds (90x210) toilet, sink, hairdryer, towels and toiletries. Our comfortable living area with large oak table and kitchen is an ideal place to have meals together, relax in the evening or spend the afternoon. Folding doors give you entrance to the beautiful terrace and the adjoining garden.

The kitchen has everything you need from pots and pans to a large 5-pit gas stove, toaster, a refrigerator, microwave and large oven. All the beds are made and towels and toiletries are taken care of during your stay. Baby beds and chairs are available on request.

Surroundings

Because of her VOC history Enkhuizen has several places worth seeing. Some of them are the Drom, the Harbor and the city Centre. Enkhuizen is also ideal as starting point to visit Bataviastad or the Bataviawerf in Lelystad, the historical city Hoorn, the Cheese market in Alkmaar or the fishing villages Volendam, Marken or Edam

Het Zuiderzeemuseum

Open air museum that lets you see, feel and taste how people of the Zuiderzee lived during the 1880 till 1930. More information: www.zuiderzeemuseum.nl

Sprookjeswonderland

Walking past several fairy tails this fantasy park has a children's farm, a castle with a show called Violinde, playground, boats for children a maze, birdhouse and a lot more. More information: www.sprookjeswonderland.nl

Historical triangle

Discover the history of the Zuiderzee with the historical steam train. More information: www.museumstoomtram.nl

Bike and Hike routes

The Westfriese Omringdijk is a beautiful 126 km route crossing the countryside of North Holland. More information: www.westfrieseomringdijk.nl. More routes: www.vvvenkhuizen.nl www.routeyou.com.

Streekbos

Next door lies recreational area Het Streekbos where you can swim, go boating, fishing, walking, cycle, eat and drink or watch the stars in the planetarium. More information: www.recreatieschapwestfriesland.nl www.volkssterrenwachtorion.nl
